Project: 
Decrypting Hashed Codes and Understanding Hashing Concepts

Objective:
To decrypt hashed codes using various hashing algorithms like SHA-1, SHA-2 (including  SHA-256), and MD5. 
Additionally, we explore hashing concepts such as salting to understand its impact on hash security.

Hashing Basics - Understand the purpose and importance of hashing in cybersecurity.
Hashing Algorithms - Learn about common hashing algorithms like SHA-1, SHA-2, and MD5. 
Decryption Challenge - Attempt to decrypt provided hashed codes using online tools or custom scripts. 
Salting Concept - Explore the concept of salting and its role in hash security.
