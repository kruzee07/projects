Decrypting Hashed Codes

Introduction

Topic Introduction: Welcome, everyone, to our presentation on decrypting hashed codes and understanding hashing concepts.

Understanding Hashing Basics

Purpose of Hashing: Hashing converts data into a fixed-size string, ensuring data integrity and authenticity.

Importance: Vital in protecting passwords, digital signatures, and verifying data integrity.

Key Properties: Deterministic, unique hashes, and resistant to collisions.

Exploring the Salting Concept

Definition: Salting adds random data to input before hashing.

Role in Security: Enhances security by making each hash unique, preventing rainbow table attacks.
